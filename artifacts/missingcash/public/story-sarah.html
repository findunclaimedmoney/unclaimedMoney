"I nearly dropped my phone when I saw the balance. It had been sitting there for years while life got busy."

Sarah spent much of her working life in regional Queensland — nursing, then healthcare administration, with a short period working interstate in her early thirties. She'd always known super was being paid but, like most Australians, assumed it was all going to the same place.

It wasn't. And the account she'd forgotten about had been compounding quietly for over twenty years.

A Chance Discovery

Sarah found MissingCash through a Facebook post shared in a local community group. She wasn't expecting to find anything significant — she figured if there was money out there, someone would have told her. But she followed the checklist anyway.

The ATO portal showed an account she'd opened through a hospital she'd worked at briefly in the late nineties. At the time she left, she'd never thought to transfer it. The fund had continued to receive investment earnings for two decades.
