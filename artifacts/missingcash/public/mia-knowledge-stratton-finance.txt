COMMON FINANCE QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS
--------------------------------------

Q: What credit score do I need?
A: Stratton works with 40+ lenders, including some who can help people with less-than-perfect credit. It's worth speaking to Erin to find out your options.

Q: How quickly can I get approved?
A: Car loans can sometimes be approved same day. Home loans typically take longer — 2 to 4 weeks depending on the lender and complexity.

Q: Does getting a quote affect my credit score?
A: Getting an indicative repayment estimate online does NOT affect your credit score. A formal credit application does leave a footprint, but Stratton manages this carefully.

Q: Can I refinance my existing loan?
A: Yes. Stratton can help you refinance car loans, personal loans, and home loans to potentially get a better rate.

Q: Can self-employed people get loans?
A: Yes. Stratton has lenders who specialise in self-employed applicants. You may need to provide tax returns or BAS statements.

Q: What is a novated lease?
A: A novated lease is a three-way arrangement between you, your employer, and a finance company. Your car repayments come from your pre-tax salary, which can save you money on income tax. It typically includes all running costs in one regular payment.

Q: What's the difference between a car loan and a novated lease?
A: A car loan means you own the car immediately (or at the end). A novated lease is a salary packaging arrangement through your employer. Both have pros and cons depending on your situation.

Q: Can I get finance for a business vehicle?
A: Yes — Stratton offers commercial finance and asset finance for business purposes.
