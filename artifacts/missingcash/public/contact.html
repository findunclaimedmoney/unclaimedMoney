Common Questions

Is the search really free?
Yes. The basic search using our checklist is completely free. The Premium Guide ($4.99) provides step-by-step claiming instructions for every register, but the initial search itself costs nothing.
How long does it take to claim money once found?
It depends on the register. Super consolidation through myGov can take a few days. State revenue claims may take 2–6 weeks. ASIC share claims are usually processed within 4–8 weeks.
Do I need a financial adviser?
For most unclaimed money — super, bank accounts, shares — you can reclaim it yourself directly through the relevant government portal. No adviser needed. Our guide walks you through every step.
