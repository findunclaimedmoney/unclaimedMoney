Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Who We Are

MissingCash (ABN 52 347 989 391) operates the website missingcash.com.au. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact details — name, email address, and phone number when you submit a form on our site.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, browser type, and device information, collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Payment information — when you purchase our Premium Guide, payment is processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your card details.
  • Form submissions — any information you voluntarily provide through our contact or finance enquiry forms.

We do not collect sensitive information such as tax file numbers, superannuation account numbers, or government ID details. All searches for lost money are conducted by you directly through official government portals.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide support
  • Deliver purchased products (e.g. the Premium Guide)
  • Pass finance enquiry details to our referral partners (with your explicit consent)
  • Improve the website and user experience using anonymised analytics data
  • Comply with our legal obligations

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service providers — including Stripe (payments), FormSubmit (form processing), and Google (analytics).
  • Referral partners — if you submit a finance enquiry, your details are shared with the relevant financial services partner (such as Stratton Finance) for the purpose of following up on your enquiry. This is disclosed at the point of form submission.
  • Legal authorities — where required by law.

5. Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects data such as pages viewed and time spent on site. This data is anonymised and cannot be used to identify you personally. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access. Our site is served over HTTPS. Payment transactions are encrypted and handled by Stripe, which is PCI DSS compliant.

7. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for up to 12 months for support and follow-up purposes. Analytics data is retained according to Google's standard retention policies. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Complain about a breach of your privacy

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@missingcash.com.au.

9. Complaints

If you believe we have breached your privacy, you can contact us directly. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be published on this page with a revised date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
